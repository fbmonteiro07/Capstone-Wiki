The accelerated-computing platform that sits at the center of the AI buildout — GPUs, CPUs (Grace/Vera), NVLink + Spectrum-X/InfiniBand networking, and the CUDA software moat sold as full-stack "AI factories." Data Center is ~90%+ of revenue ($75B of $82B in Q1 FY27); the rest is Gaming, Pro Viz, and Automotive/physical AI. From Q1 FY27 NVDA re-segmented Data Center into Hyperscale (~50%) and ACIE (AI clouds, industrial, enterprise, sovereign, ~50%) plus a new Edge Computing platform. Whole thesis is leverage to hyperscaler + sovereign + neocloud capex, where NVDA captures the compute, networking, and increasingly the CPU. The scale-up network is core to the moat: NVLink is ~the entire scale-up market today and NVDA is expected to remain the majority of a market growing $4B (2024) → ~$17B by 2029, 34% CAGR (MS Scale-up primer); on compute NVDA is modeled holding ~70% share into C28, with per-GW revenue economics nearly 2x its closest peer (UBS 2026 overview).
