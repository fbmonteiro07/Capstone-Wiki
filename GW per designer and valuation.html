Notes

  1. Model usage: Capstone NVDA model supplies NVDA figures only (GW, revenue, units); all custom-silicon client figures (Google / Anthropic / OpenAI / META GW and AI revenue) come from the Capstone AVGO model.
  2. Basis differences: SemiAnalysis chip-implied GW = shipments × all-in chip power (IT load incl. server/networking overhead, no facility PUE), booked in shipment year. Capstone NVDA GW = deployed capacity grossed up ~1.5× (PUE 1.1 + 35% other) — closer to grid power. Capstone AVGO = client datacenter GW served by AVGO silicon.
  3. NVDA 2027: Capstone GW (24.8) > SemiAnalysis (18.2) but Capstone compute revenue ($518bn) < SA-implied ($572bn) — Capstone assumes more chips (7.6M vs ~6.0M ex-China) at lower blended ASP and higher W/chip.
  4. AVGO 2027: SA-implied XPU revenue ($163bn) is +82% vs Capstone ASIC ($90bn), driven by Anthropic premium system-level ASPs ($33.8–37k/chip) and the OpenAI Titan ramp ($44bn). Capstone AVGO carries OpenAI at only $13bn compute in 2027. 2026 is nearly identical ($55.4bn vs $51.7bn).
  5. Capstone NVDA model years treated as calendar (FY ~ CY+1 offset ignored); AVGO model 2025E–2027E columns used as-is.
