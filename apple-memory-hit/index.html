Method & caveats

$/GB[q] = $/GB[q-1] × (1 + QoQ%[q]). Market track ≈ server/contract DDR5 & commodity NAND proxy; Apple track = LTA-cushioned realized price (lower steps in the melt-up quarters, converging to the ~$6/GB DRAM floor). Mobile LPDDR5/NAND $/GB is not disclosed at device level — these are proxy anchors calibrated so the market DRAM track lands near >$16/GB ($2/Gb) by mid-2026 and the Apple track near the UBS floor by 2027. Treat absolute $ as illustrative; the shape and the relative Apple-vs-market gap are the analytical point. All numbers attributed above; the model is the synthesis, not a primary source.
