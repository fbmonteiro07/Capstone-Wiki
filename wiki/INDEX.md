## Notes / fidelity caveats

- **Transcript fidelity varies by source:** IR-CDN FactSet/S&P PDFs = full verbatim (UBER, MSFT, GOOG, NFLX, BKNG, CSCO, AMZN, AAPL, ASML, GEV, COHR). Third-party-only where WebFetch refused verbatim = **paraphrased / [GAP] markers** (NOW, parts of LITE/GLW/ORCL/CEG). Prepared-remarks-only (no public Q&A) = **SPOT**, parts of **ASML/BKNG**.
- **Partial coverage** (Cloudflare-walled IR + paywalled third parties): CEG, WMB, VST, ORCL, PWR, TLN.
- **20-F filers** (no 10-K/10-Q): ASML, SPOT, TSM, TM. **ENR GY (Siemens Energy):** no SEC filings.
- Mega-caps (AAPL/MSFT/GOOG/AMZN/META/NFLX/BKNG) hold no investor day → no decks (correct).
- Transcript scope = last ~8 quarters (earnings calls). Decks = investor/analyst days, last 5y.
- Known thin transcript gaps (low public availability): TM (Toyota — no English transcripts), AAOI/AGX (small-cap), TLN (relisted 2024). NVDA limited to 5 (FY25 quarters not public). Quarters not yet occurred as of run date are not gaps.
- **Equity calls (`_equity_calls/`):** thematic sell-side / expert / management calls (mirror of `E:/equity_calls_transcripts`), auto-attributed per ticker by scanning each call's text + filename. A call appears under a ticker if its name/symbol is mentioned ≥3x or is in the filename; `(n)` = mention count. Many calls are multi-company, so the same call can appear under several tickers; thematic calls touching no covered ticker are not listed here (see the full archive index).
- **Briefings (`_briefings/by-ticker/`):** the daily email briefings (morning TMTB digest + company-specific email/X) are dense multi-name digests, so instead of listing every dated file per ticker they are rolled up into one chronological file per name — morning table rows (PT/rating/EPS actions) + `**TICKER**` callouts, and the `### TICKER` per-company sections from company-specific. Twitter-only briefings excluded. Rebuild with `build_briefings_rollup.py`.
