## Snapshot
Palantir builds AI-powered data-integration and decision-intelligence platforms for government and commercial customers, anchored by Foundry (commercial) and Gotham (government). The bull thesis centers on Palantir's ontology layer — a proprietary data model brokers assert AI labs cannot effectively replicate — marketed as an "action engine" with a claimed 5-year moat. AIPCon (its customer AI conference) has been the primary narrative catalyst in May–June 2026. The stock hit a 12-month relative low in mid-May 2026 (~$146, down from ~$207 in Nov 2025) before recovering through June. Consensus is overwhelmingly constructive on momentum but split on valuation.

## Debate / thesis
- **Bull — ontology moat is durable and not competitively threatened:** UBS (Keirstead) post-NDR/AIPCon argues AI labs are "not effectively competing" with the ontology layer; the largest SI partner frames it as a "5-year moat / action engine." Customers optimizing token use via Palantir's free Evolve offering (90% customer adoption in 3 weeks) (UBS, 2026-06-15/16). BofA (Perez Mora) more aggressive — "compounding ontology" and Apollo addressing AI-native cybersecurity.
- **Bear / debate — valuation and AI-disruption pressure:** PLTR hit a "12-month relative low" in mid-May alongside AI-disruption-basket pressure (2026-05-14). The broader "AI software ROI / messier margin math" debate (The Information) included PLTR (FundaAI, 2026-05-11). DB's Hold at $200 — maintained across Q3 2025, Q4 2025, and Q1 2026 beats — is the bear anchor (a constructive Hold, not a genuine bear; full coverage universe not captured).
